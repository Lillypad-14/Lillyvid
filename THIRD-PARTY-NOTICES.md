## Pokémon Showdown data, sprites, and animations (Lillypad Go)

The Lillypad Go mini-game derives its species/move data, creature spritesheets, fx
sprites, and battle-animation choreography from **Pokémon Showdown**:

- Source: https://github.com/smogon/pokemon-showdown and
  https://github.com/smogon/pokemon-showdown-client
- Move animation definitions (`battle-animations-moves`): **CC0-1.0** (public domain),
  per the file's own license header.
- Battle animation engine (`battle-animations`, whose projection/easing model
  `Phone/Apps/LillypadGo/MoveAnim.cs` reimplements): **MIT**, per the same header.
- `Assets/pokemon/moveanims.json` is generated by executing the CC0 animation
  definitions and recording their keyframes (see `Phone/Apps/LillypadGo/tools/`).
- Gym badge emblems (`Assets/pokemon/badges/*.png`) are the type icons from the
  Pokémon Showdown client (`play.pokemonshowdown.com/sprites/types/`).
